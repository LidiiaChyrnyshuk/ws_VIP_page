How many bets do I need to place to earn 1 point?
The number of bets required to earn 1 point depends on the game type and the current exchange rate. Points are calculated using the following formula:
Points Amount = (Bet Amount × Bet Wager) / Exchange Rate
Where:
◦ Bet Wager - a coefficient that depends on the type of game you are playing.
◦ Exchange Rate - the current actual rate of 1 WEFT.
You can view all Bet Wagers in the Play to Earn section on the Analytics page.
For example, to earn 1 point at Slots you need to bet ~15 USDT, in Live Games ~150 USDT.
